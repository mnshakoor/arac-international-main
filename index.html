Who We Are

Advancing Sustainable Peace Through Principled Action

ARAC International is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to advocating for global security, human rights, and peacebuilding efforts. Our approach to addressing complex international challenges is to advocate for the institutions and structures that foster Positive Peace.

We believe that sustainable peace and security can be attained through the advancement of human rights and proactive engagement in conflict resolution. We strive to effect enduring transformations by empowering local communities, influencing global policies, and nurturing a culture that prioritizes peace.

Framework

Advocates for Conflict Prevention & Mediation with Positive Peace

The Positive Peace Framework, developed by the Institute for Economics and Peace, identifies the attitudes, institutions, and structures that create and sustain peaceful societies.

1
Early Warning Systems

Identifying and addressing potential sources of tension before they escalate into full-blown conflicts through systematic monitoring and structured analytic frameworks.

2
Mediation Integration

Facilitating dialogue between conflicting parties and helping them reach mutually acceptable solutions, preventing further escalation of violence and building sustainable peace.

3
Global Framework Advocacy

Advocating for the integration of conflict prevention programs into international frameworks to ensure coordinated, strategic, and resourced approaches to peacebuilding.
